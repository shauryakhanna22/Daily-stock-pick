### #10 - AMAT: Applied Materials
**Sector:** AI/Semiconductors | **Cap:** Large Cap (~$190B) | **Score:** 4/4 | **Conviction:** MEDIUM

**Why now:** Applied Materials is the largest semiconductor equipment company by revenue and the primary beneficiary of TSMC's N2/A16 node ramp and Intel's IDM 2.0 buildout. **Key risk:** Semiconductor equipment cycles are lumpy; if leading-edge capex moderates in 2027, order books compress rapidly. China export restrictions remain an overhang.
