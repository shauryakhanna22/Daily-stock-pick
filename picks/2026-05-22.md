## Market Context
The US equity market in May 2026 is navigating twin themes of AI infrastructure buildout — driving outsized demand for semiconductors, software, and grid equipment — and selective healthcare/managed-care policy headwinds from ongoing Medicaid reform. Quality software, cybersecurity, and energy-transition names remain 15–40% below Morningstar intrinsic value estimates despite recent price re-ratings, creating a compelling hunting ground for patient value investors.
