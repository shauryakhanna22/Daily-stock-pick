# Daily Stock Picks - 2026-05-17
**Analyst:** Claude (claude-sonnet-4-6) | **Framework:** Growth + Quality + Valuation

## Market Context
US equities enter mid-May 2026 trading approximately 14% below Morningstar's aggregate fair value estimates — a dislocation rooted in Q1 tariff volatility and Fed rate-path recalibration that has created asymmetric entry points across AI infrastructure, cybersecurity, and energy-transition leaders whose long-term compounding power is obscured by near-term macro noise. With the 10-year Treasury yield anchored near 4.3%, stocks with FCF yields above that threshold and double-digit earnings growth present the most compelling risk-adjusted value the market has offered since 2022.

**Sector:** Technology / AI Infrastructure / Semiconductors | **Cap:** Mega Cap ($816B) | **Conviction:** HIGH

**Why undervalued now:** At a forward P/E of just 12x, Micron remains one of the cheapest pure-play AI infrastructure names despite having committed HBM capacity through 2026 and analysts forecasting 111% revenue growth in fiscal 2026.
