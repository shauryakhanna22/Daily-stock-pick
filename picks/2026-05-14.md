**Sector:** Technology / AI Infrastructure | **Cap:** Mega Cap ($906B) | **Conviction:** SPECULATIVE

**Why undervalued now:** Micron is the primary beneficiary of the AI memory supercycle via High-Bandwidth Memory (HBM), with revenue expected to jump 111% in fiscal 2026. At $793 per share, the forward P/E of approximately 12x is dramatically low for a company with that growth trajectory — peer semiconductor stocks trade at 25–35x forward earnings. The analyst Strong Buy consensus is near-unanimous: 27 buys, 3 holds, 0 sells. Mizuho raised its target to $740 on May 6, and the bull case target stands at $1,000.

**Future potential:** HBM is the critical memory component in every AI training and inference cluster — NVIDIA H100/H200, AMD MI300, and all custom accelerators require it.
