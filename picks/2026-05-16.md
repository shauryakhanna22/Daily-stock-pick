### #2 - MU: Micron Technology
**Sector:** Technology / AI Infrastructure (Semiconductors) | **Cap:** Mega Cap ($820B) | **Conviction:** HIGH

**Why undervalued now:** Micron trades at a forward P/E of just 12x while analysts expect 111% revenue growth in fiscal 2026 driven by HBM (high-bandwidth memory) for AI accelerators — implying a PEG ratio below 0.15x, among the cheapest in the S&P 500 relative to its growth rate. The fundamental case for undervaluation is compelling even as the stock has outpaced the near-term analyst consensus mean.

**Future potential:** Every major AI chip (NVIDIA H200/B100, AMD Instinct, Google TPU) requires HBM; Micron is one of three global suppliers. HBM3E gross margins exceed traditional DRAM by 3x, driving a structural re-rating in earnings power. Bull-case analyst targets reach $1,000.

**Key risk:** Memory is cyclical; if AI capex spending slows or Samsung/SK Hynix oversupply emerges, prices could correct sharply. Analyst mean target ($585) is currently below market price, reflecting near-term caution on the cycle.

### #8 - PFE: Pfizer
**Sector:** Healthcare | **Cap:** Large Cap ($145B) | **Conviction:** MEDIUM

**Why undervalued now:** Pfizer has been beaten down post-pandemic, but its fundamental pipeline is deeply underappreciated at current prices near 5-year lows. A ~6.5% dividend yield, forward P/E near 8x, and a late-stage pipeline spanning oncology, vaccines, and oral GLP-1 (danuglipron) make it one of the most asymmetric large-cap healthcare plays.

**Key risk:** Patent cliff on COVID-era products; competition from Novo Nordisk and Eli Lilly in GLP-1 obesity; IRA drug price negotiation pressure on oncology franchises.

### #9 - FAF: First American Financial Corporation
**Sector:** Financials / Fintech | **Cap:** Mid Cap ($6.7B) | **Conviction:** MEDIUM

**Key risk:** Housing affordability constraints may limit transaction volumes even with rate normalisation; potential regulatory scrutiny on title insurance pricing; interest rate sensitivity in the investment portfolio.
